How it started

Pawprint was created in November 2015 at the global entrepreneurial event called Startup Weekend in Columbia, SC to help connect dog owners with services, activities, and local dining options.

Mission

We know how much you love your dog and that is why Pawprint can help make it easier to bring your furry friend with you when you go out.

Whether you're new to town or looking for a new restaurant to try, Pawprint can show you a variety of veterinarian offices, groomers, social events, dog parks, or a fun place to grab a brew with ratings and reviews from other dog owners

Be sure to share the love and leave a review on Pawprint.
